Why Darling?

Why is the title named Darling's 17 and not Jaanu's 17 even though I call you jaanu all the time... there's a reason I've never properly opened up about.

Back when I had started liking you, I genuinely had no idea that you liked me too. One day I casually called you darling... then you started calling me darling too. That one word made me ridiculously happy and gave me hope that maybe you liked me back.

I even told Afroz and he laughed saying you definitely liked me. Looking back it was just one word... but somehow it became ours.

Even today I call you baby, jaanu, shona and sweetheart... but darling will always remind me of where everything started. I hope years from now, even if we have a hundred nicknames, darling still makes us smile because we'll remember exactly how it all began.

I love youuu my darling... always.

Our Story

Where do I even start? 😭 I guess it all started way before either of us knew what was about to happen. Back in 4th grade, I remember thinking you were really pretty. It wasn't love or anything... just one of those tiny childhood crushes that you don't think much about.

Then life happened... and in 7th grade I found out my best friend was dating you. I won't lie... I used to listen to Treat You Better a lot back then 😭. Looking back now, it's actually funny because I had no idea that one day I'd end up sending that song to you. After that, life moved on and we slowly drifted. You were still my friend... but nothing more than that.

Then came 9th grade. We ended up in the same class, and somehow... without either of us planning it... we became best friends. That's when everything started changing. I found myself texting you way more than anyone else. I'd randomly wait for your replies, think about you throughout the day, and somehow every little thing reminded me of you. Honestly... not much has changed because I still do all of that 😭.

One day, I randomly called you "darling." Don't ask me where the confidence came from because I genuinely have no idea 😂. It just slipped out. And then... you called me darling too. That one word made me happier than it probably should have. I told Afroz about it, and I still remember him laughing and saying, "Bro... she definitely likes you." Then that idiot went ahead and told you that I liked you because I was taking you calling me "darling" in a romantic way 😭. Looking back now... that whole situation was so funny, but I think that's when "darling" quietly became our thing.

After months of overthinking, hoping, waiting and trying to figure out if you felt the same way... I finally gathered the courage to ask you.

15th December 2023. 12:00 AM.

One text. One question.

Then came the longest wait of my life 😭. You took your own sweet time to reply, and every second felt like forever. But then you finally said yes... and that's the moment our story truly began.

From 15th December 2023 to today, we've laughed until our stomachs hurt, argued over the dumbest things, annoyed each other more times than I can count, made up after fights, celebrated little wins together, and created memories I'll probably carry for the rest of my life.

Have we fought? More times than we'd like to admit 😭. But the one thing I've realised is that no fight has ever been stronger than what we have. Somehow, no matter how upset we get, we always find our way back to each other. I think that's my favourite thing about us.

We laugh like best friends.

We care for each other like a couple.

We fight like siblings.

And somehow... all three of those things make us us.

I genuinely wouldn't want our story to be perfect. I'd rather have this one... because it's real. It's messy sometimes, it's emotional sometimes, it's full of random inside jokes, late-night conversations, unnecessary arguments, "good morning" texts, "have you eaten?" reminders, and a million little moments that probably don't mean much to anyone else... but mean everything to me.

So if someone ever asks me what my favourite love story is...

It'll always be ours.

And hopefully... this is only the beginning. I love you so much, my darling. Here's to every memory we've already made... and to all the ones that are still waiting for us. ❤️
